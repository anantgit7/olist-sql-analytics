Aim / Purpose:
The objective of this dashboard is to provide a consolidated and interactive view of business performance across sales, orders, customer behavior, product categories, and geography. The dashboard combines KPIs, trend analysis, and visual breakdowns to enable monitoring of revenue, order volume, spending patterns, and regional demand. It allows users to explore data across multiple time periods and dimensions, supporting faster and more informed decision-making.

Dashboard Components
1) KPI Cards with Embedded Trend Lines
1.1 Total Sales (with Quarterly Sales Trend)

Displays total revenue for the selected period, along with a quarterly trend line that highlights growth patterns and seasonal performance.

1.2 Average Order Value (with Quarterly AOV Trend)

Shows average order value and how customer spending changes across quarters, helping to understand efficiency and purchase behavior.

1.3 Total Orders (with Quarterly Order Trend)

Represents total order volume and its quarterly evolution, revealing spikes and declines in customer activity.

2) Date Slicer (All Years, 2016, 2017, 2018)

Allows filtering of visuals by year to compare performance across different periods and analyze yearly trends.

3) Payment Method Distribution (Pie Chart)

Shows percentage share of payment types (credit card, boleto, voucher, debit card), helping to understand customer preferences and payment mix.

4) Sales by Month-Year (Line Chart)

Illustrates monthly sales across the timeline, revealing seasonality, growth patterns, and demand fluctuations for forecasting.

5) Top 10 Categories by Sales (Bar Chart)

Highlights the most revenue-generating product categories, enabling prioritization for marketing, promotions, and inventory planning.

6) Geographical Sales Map (Bubble Map)

Visualizes sales contribution by Brazilian states, where bubble size represents sales volume, helping identify strong regions and expansion opportunities.
